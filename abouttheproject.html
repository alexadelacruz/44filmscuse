This is 44 Films!

By Syracuse University

The Sports Media Center at Syracuse University's S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications, in conjunction with the Syracuse Department of Athletics, is launching 44 FILMS. The project is focused on short form non-fiction narrative filmmaking that puts the students at the forefront of every aspect of production, with the guidance of faculty and professional staff.

A selected group of students will lead the projects from ideation to final distribution as a collective, with support from the Sports Media Center faculty, including Jordan Kligerman, Visiting Assistant Professor, and Olivia Stomski, Director of the Sports Media Center and professor of practice, and Department of Athletics staff members. 44 Films will produce up to eight documentary shorts during the 2021-22 academic year, and potential "specials" that work in conjunction with the films. The cinematic style documentary shorts will show the diversity of Syracuse University Athletics from a student-athlete, coaching staff and historical perspective.

ASSOCIATION

44 Films is a student ran film production company for Syracuse University Newhouse Public School of Communications
